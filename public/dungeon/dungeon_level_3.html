Goblin Barricade

A big barricade was built here made of spears, table legs, and other improvised materials. 1 vault weaver is here, just finishing to absorb a corpse, trowing it's silk around the room. All movement inside the room is reduced by 3 tiles. The weaver will finish absorbing the corpse in one turn and become a red weaver. As soon as it eats the corpse the adventurers feel a wave o nausea, and anxiety, as it was emanating from the orb.
